One word for Raleway? Elegant. It has unique characters like the criss-crossed w and an l with a tail. It's intended for headings and other large size usage.

It features both old style and lining numerals, standard and discretionary ligatures, a pretty complete set of diacritics, as well as a stylistic alternates.

It also has a sister family, Raleway Dots.

More information can be found at theleagueofmoveabletype.com and impallari.com

To contribute to the project, visit github.com/impallari/Raleway
