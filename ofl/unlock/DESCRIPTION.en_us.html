Unlock is a geometric typeface with vertical stress, short descenders and a significant "x" height. Basic shapes from rectangles, some with rounded corners (the only presence of curves), completely rectangular counterpunches and innovative solutions for the joints, gives Unlock a unique style.

Their rough appearance, is reminiscent of the old types of wood, although the design of many of the signs we associate it with is also a futuristic design.

Unlock is ideal for the composition of headlines and short texts but its design provides for the possibility of using it in small sizes.

A script type by Eduardo Tunni. More documentation can be found at www.tipo.net.ar
