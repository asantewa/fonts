Formal Scripts are great typefaces to suggest elegance, quality, refinement and luxury. They are used a lot in print publishing, but you don't see many formal scripts used on the web. Mostly because their tiny hairlines break and disappear when used at small sizes on the screen.

Also, they usually have long ascenders and descenders, and the lowercase letters look too small when compared to most sans or serif fonts. Moreover, they tend to be quite condensed, making them even more difficult to read.

That's why Petit Formal Script was designed: a script type specifically tailored for web use, that can survive being set even as small as 13px
