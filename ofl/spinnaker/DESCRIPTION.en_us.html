Spinnaker is based on French and UK lettering found on posters made for travelling by ship. It is a low contrast and slightly wide sans serif design, with a youthful sense of whimsiness combined with the expected utility that a sans serif commonly offers. Spinnaker is suitable for use in medium to large sizes.

Updated in January 2012.

Source files are available from Google Code. To contribute to the project contact Eben Sorkin.
