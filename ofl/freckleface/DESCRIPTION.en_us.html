Freckle Face draws its inspiration from Pillbury's Funny Face drink mix packages. Designer Brain J loved these drink mixes when he was a kid, not only because of the great flavors, but also for the fun packaging. He renewed the love affair later by loving the lettering too!

Designed by Brian J. Bonislawsky for Astigmatic (AOETI). To contribute to the project contact Brian J. Bonislawsky.
