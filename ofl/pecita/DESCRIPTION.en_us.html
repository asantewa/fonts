Pecita is a font that was created from the designer’s handwriting. The raw material of Pecita is a scan of the designer’s handwriting with its flaws and charms. The font is available as OpenType (.OTF) from pecita.eu. It is coded with full Unicode glyphs customary in Latin and pan European languages, the phonetic alphabet, katakana, and many symbols. It includes many ligatures.

Version 2.5 represents the achievement of key objectives: all the Latin characters used nowadays, katakana, all Unicode characters up to 0 × 02AF, ligatures enough for a realistic handwritten record.
