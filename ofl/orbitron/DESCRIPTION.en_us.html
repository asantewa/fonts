Orbitron is a geometric sans-serif typeface intended for display purposes. It was designed with the intention of giving graphic designers working on projects set in the future, alternatives to typefaces like Eurostile or Bank Gothic. In futuristic sci-fi movies and movie posters, Orbitron can be used on spaceship exteriors, space station signage, monopolistic corporate branding, uniforms featuring aerodynamic shoulder pads etc. Orbitron can also be used for futuristic movie posters.
